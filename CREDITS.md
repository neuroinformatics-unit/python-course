# Credits

## Exercise inspiration

Several of the small function-writing practice exercises in Quests 2, 3, and 4 (lesson IDs prefixed `cb-`) are inspired by problems from **[CodingBat](https://codingbat.com/)** by Nick Parlante (Stanford). Prompts have been rewritten in our own words and the test inputs are our own, but the underlying problem ideas and the function names are credited to CodingBat.

If you enjoy the practice exercises, CodingBat has many more (Python and Java) — go work through them at https://codingbat.com/python.

## Course material

The curriculum itself is adapted from the Sydney Informatics Hub Python intro course (slide decks in `materials/`).
