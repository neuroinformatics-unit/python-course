# Why you shouldn’t use AI to code while you are a beginner

- You need the skills to comprehend what the AI is saying to you
- Sometimes you reach a problem the AI cannot solve.. What then?
- To use AI effectively you need to be able to articulate the problem and what exactly it is you want to do. This is fine when someone has written out the problem as an assignment, but in the real world you will have to articulate the problems yourself!
